Dr. D Y Patil College of Engineering & Innovation Library

Welcome to the library of Dr. D Y Patil College of Engineering & Innovation, where knowledge meets innovation. Our library is committed to providing a comprehensive range of resources and services to support the educational and research needs of our students and faculty. Explore our extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources tailored for engineering and innovation studies. Our skilled staff is here to assist you in navigating the wealth of information available.
