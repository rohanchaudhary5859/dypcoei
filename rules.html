Library Rules and Regulations

  1. Identity Card is compulsory for getting access to the library.
  2. Enter your name and sign in the register kept at the entrance counter before entering the library.
  3. Library borrower cards are not transferable.
    The borrower is responsible for the books borrowed on his/her card.
  4. Registration should be done to become a library member prior to using the library resources.
  5. Student/Staff could not be able to submit the book on the same day.
  6. Fine of 2 Rs/day will be charged after the due date of renew/return.
  7. Silence to be maintained.
  8. No discussion permitted inside the library.
  9. Using Mobile phones and audio instruments with or without speaker or headphone is strictly prohibited in the library premises.
  10. Show the books and other materials which are being taken out of the library to the staff at the entrance counter.

Loss of cards:
Loss of borrower card should be reported to the librarian. Duplicate card may be issued against formal application and Rs.50 fine.
